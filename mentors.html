Dr. Ahmed Abukmail, Ph.D.

Abukmail@UHCL.edu

Dr. Ahmed Abukmail is an associate professor of computer science at the University of Houston-Clear Lake. He is also the computer science program chair. He hold a Masters and Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Florida, and a Bachelors of Science from Ohio State University. His areas of interest are Mobile and Pervasive Computing, Machine Learning, and Computer Science Education. He is a professional member of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). In addition to his over 15 years of academic experience, Dr. Abukmail has 5 years of industry experience in software development and system administration.
